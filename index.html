Hey there! I’m Christine Vargas — a data analyst who believes dashboards should not only inform, but inspire.

With a background in HR operations, project coordination, and business intelligence, I specialize in turning raw data into meaningful stories. Whether I’m analyzing Netflix trends or building people analytics dashboards in Power BI or Tableau, I’m all about making complex info simple, visual, and actionable.

I work with tools like Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Excel, and Workday, blending technical skill with a creative approach to design. My favorite projects? The ones where data actually solves a problem — or makes someone say, “Whoa, that’s cool.”

Outside of dashboards, you’ll usually find me gaming (RPGs and cozy sims are my jam), exploring new ways to streamline everything, or hanging out with my two mischievous Sphynx cats, Cleopatra and Vesper, who are basically my project managers.

Take a look around — and feel free to reach out if you want to chat dashboards, data, or your favorite gaming side quest!
